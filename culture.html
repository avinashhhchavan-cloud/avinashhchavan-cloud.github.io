Festivals of Solapur

Siddheshwar Yatra

One of the largest festivals celebrated in honor of Shri Siddheshwar. Devotees participate in religious ceremonies and processions.

Ashadhi Wari

A famous pilgrimage where devotees walk long distances to reach Pandharpur while singing devotional songs.

Local Traditions

The culture of Solapur reflects a mix of Marathi and Kannada traditions. People celebrate religious festivals and community gatherings throughout the year.
